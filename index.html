Running

The skill of running includes jogging, sprinting, chasing, dodging
and evading. All of these are important to many games (e.g. tag),
sports (e.g. athletics) and everyday activities (e.g. running
to get to school on time).

Walking

For example, regular brisk walking can help you: Maintain a
healthy weight and lose body fat. Prevent or manage various conditions,
including heart disease, stroke, high blood pressure, cancer
and type 2 diabetes. Improve cardiovascular fitness. Strengthen your bones
and muscles.

Jogging

Regularly jogging can help you lose weight, especially
if you also modify your diet. Jogging can also help you improve your heart
health and immune system, reduce insulin resistance, cope
with stress and depression, and maintain flexibility as you age.
